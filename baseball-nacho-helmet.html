MLB Nacho Helmets

Which ones are homeruns

and which ones struck out

So why did I decide to try all the baseball nacho helmets?

My boyfriend and I love baseball. I mean love baseball. We go to at least 10 or more games a year and we recently moved near White Sox Park. (#iwillnevercallitguaranteedratefield <-- follow on Instagram).

In fact one of our first dates was a baseball game and we shared a nacho helmet. It seemed really silly, but I wondered how many other baseball stadiums have them. As the conversation continued (with a few more Old Styles, too), we decided it would be fun to try to collect them from every stadium that offered one.

This is still a work in progress, but we're trying! Check back for updates!
